IMM Programming Tutorial

Brief explanations for basics and FAQs about IMM/IME.

 

Before reading the explanations here, please remember that not all the IMEs support all the IMM features. Some IMEs may not respond to your request in some cases. To confirm how an IME behaves generally toward your operation, testing with Microsoft IME 95/97/98/2000 is recommended for the present. They have been doing much to establish and verify the Windows' IME specification.

Sample codes in this topic leave out error handling. Please don't forget to handle the possible errors by yourself.
